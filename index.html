The five headaches Rakeez takes off your desk.

"Our people are spread across a dozen branches."

One system for every branch

Attendance, payroll, and people for all your branches in one place — with numbers rolled up per branch and for the whole company.

"Half my team lives on commission."

Commissions, done right

Base salary, sales commissions, and bonuses calculated together and paid correctly — no side spreadsheet, no month-end panic.

"My agents are never at a desk."

Check in from any site

Agents clock in from the branch, a showroom, or a viewing — with GPS location — right from their phone.
